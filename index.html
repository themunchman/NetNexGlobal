ABOUT

Today’s CMOs are at a cross-roads. The role and stature of the CMO has dramatically expanded within the organization, moving from a purely advertising function to a growth driver. From measuring marketing results, to linking financial performances to marketing. While meeting ever-changing customer expectations is a decisive differentiating factor in competitive markets. CMOs with data, martech and customer experience will capitalize on this need in time to broaden their scope across the marketing mix.

At the CMO StraTech Summit, we combine the collective learning of CMOs from across industries and sectors to discus their best learning in the recent times and share the most promising practices. The agenda & format are aligned to relevant challenges and interests, practical and progressive ideas and strategies employed by successful pioneers.

Topics


Digital-First marketing Leaders

To keep pace with customers and competitors in today’s rapidly evolving business environment, organizations are undergoing digital transformation in multiple ways. CMOs being the custodians of overall experience & brand, find themselves focusing on leveraging technology to move beyond demand generation to a growth driven function.

Championing Data-Driven Marketing for ROI

Social media, mobile devices, analytics and cloud computing are dramatically reshaping what is possible for the modern marketer. Leveraging data to produce valuable insights to be stitched across channels to take advantage of this shift is important to maintain customer loyalty and improve satisfaction.

Changing landscape of marketing automation

Even before COVID-19 hit the world, marketing was undergoing a major digital transformation. The pandemic forced marketing and IT to work together, shrinking the collaboration gap. MarTech will continue to be one of the key focus areas for CMOs. Working towards a clear acquisition and retention strategy for tech scalability and ROI.

Omni-channel; Silver Bullet For Retention And Growth

In today’s customer-centric economy, the customer journey management strategy is a critical business requirement in order to remain competitive. Integrating multiple channels, learning customer behaviour and improving personalization helps CMOs give their brands the necessary competitive edge.
